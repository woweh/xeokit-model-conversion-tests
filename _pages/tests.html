Introduction

Periodically, we run our AEC test models through all our conversion pipelines. That action statically-generates this page of test results, where you can check if the pipelines are converting our models correctly. Click the converted files in the left column to view them in a xeokit viewer. Click the log links on the right to see what happened during their conversion.
